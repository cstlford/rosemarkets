About Rose Markets

Hi, I’m Kiana—founder of Rose Markets.

I started this company with a simple idea: premium spaces deserve premium experiences, even when it comes to snacks and beverages. Rose Markets was born from my belief that convenience shouldn’t come at the expense of quality—or style.

Based in Austin, Texas, we partner with forward-thinking businesses to provide smart, self-serve markets that feel like a natural extension of their environment. Whether it’s a boutique hotel, a modern office, or a luxury gym, our goal is the same: elevate the everyday with thoughtful, beautifully integrated service.
